# Channex Acceptable Use Policy

**Archived for:** Fine Star d.o.o. / stay.hr (May 2026)

## Prohibited uses (summary)

### Disruption

- No vulnerability probing, unauthorized access, reverse engineering, overwhelming infrastructure (robots, excessive API load)

### Wrongful activities

- No misrepresentation, privacy violations, harassment, illegal use, scraping non-public interfaces

### Inappropriate communications

- No spam, unauthorized commercial solicitation, disparaging Channex/partners

### Inappropriate content

- No IP infringement, illegal/obscene content, malware, hate speech

**Enforcement:** Channex may suspend or terminate accounts without notice for violations.
