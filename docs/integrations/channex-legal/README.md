## Key points for stay.hr (non-legal summary)

This is **not legal advice** — review with accountant/lawyer if needed.

### Commercial

- **Auto-renewal** (§7.2): subscription renews each period unless cancelled in Channex account before the billing cycle ends.
- **30-day return** (§10.1): refund within 30 days of first payment for any reason (one-time window).
- **After 30 days** (§10.3): fees generally **non-refundable**; cancel stops future charges at end of current cycle.
- **Channex termination** (§17): either party may terminate with **30 days notice**; Channex may also terminate for material breach (30 days to cure).
- **Scope of Use** (§4): billable units include properties/users — confirm **per live property** fee with Evan.
- **UK law / arbitration** (§24): disputes under UK law; binding arbitration (ICC), London if not settled in 60 days.

### Product / integration (stay.hr as PMS)

- **Whitelabel plan:** confirm with Channex whether **§6.2 “Powered by Channex”** attribution still applies to your UI or is waived under whitelabel.
- **API / Add-ons** (§13): stay.hr PMS integration is an Add-on; you indemnify Channex for third-party claims related to your integration.
- **Your Data** (§7.4): booking/ARI data you send — you grant Channex license to process for the service; you retain ownership.
- **Sensitive data** (§7.7.2): do not send unnecessary PCI/card data through Channex APIs (stay.hr already does not store cards).

### GDPR (§28 addendum)

- **Fine Star / property** = **controller** for guest personal data in bookings.
- **Channex** = **processor** for data processed to provide channel manager services.
- stay.hr should maintain its own privacy policy and DPA chain: guest → property → stay.hr → Channex → OTAs.

### Data retention (Security Policy summary)

- Bookings: deleted ~**3 months after departure**
- Credit card metadata (if any in Channex): ~**7 days after departure**
- Account data after cancellation: within **30 days**
- ARI past dates: removed daily
