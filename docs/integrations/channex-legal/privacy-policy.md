# Channex Privacy Policy

**Archived for:** Fine Star d.o.o. / stay.hr (May 2026)  
**Vendor:** Channex.io ltd (UK), GDPR commitment stated in policy

## Scope

Applies to Channex websites, SaaS products, and services accessed via devices. Does not cover third-party marketplace add-ons (separate policies).

By registering or using Channex Services you consent to collection and processing described herein.

## Information collected

- **Account/profile:** name, email, address, phone, billing, username, job title, preferences
- **Content:** data uploaded to SaaS (may include personal data in bookings)
- **Logs:** IP, browser, URLs, timestamps, locale, device IDs
- **Analytics:** feature usage, license identifier, domain, username in logs
- **Cookies / tracking:** cookies, web beacons, local storage

## How information is used

Provide and improve services, transactions, support, security, fraud prevention, marketing (opt-out available), analytics.

## Sharing

Not sold. Shared with: collaborators per your settings, service providers (hosting, payment), third-party add-ons you enable, legal compliance, business transfers, with consent.

## Data storage and security

Hosted in US, Europe, and other countries. SSL in transit. No guarantee against all breaches.

## Your choices

Opt out of promotional email; transactional emails continue. Access/update/delete via account settings or support@channex.io (response within 30 days).

## GDPR / complaints

Right to access and deletion. Legal retention (e.g. invoices 7 years). Complaints: support@channex.io; escalate to evan@channex.io. Response target under 28 days.

## Children

Not directed at under-13; data deleted if discovered.
